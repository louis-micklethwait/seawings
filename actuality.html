SEAWINGS



SeaWings comes from “wings of the sea”. This name was given to it because the company is a maritime company specializing in the collection of waste.



The company emerged on January 6, 2020 after a group of activists who saw the worrying amount of waste in the sea decided to create a “SeaWings” company in order to make the ocean blue again.



Louis Micklethwait, CEO of SeaWings will declare his project on January 20, 2020 and will very quickly receive a large number of donations allowing the company to finance their first major expeditions. The millionaire Océane Krull will also take part in this project by financing the construction of their “Manta” allowing them to collect a large quantity of plastics.

Since then, SeaWings has been taking out tons of plastics out of the sea every month and keeps receiving a lot of donation that helps the company going.
